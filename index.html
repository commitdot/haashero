Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the base subscription?

The base subscription includes a secure LPAR partition with allocated vCP, RAM, storage, and bandwidth. It provides the foundational infrastructure to run your workloads.

How does billing work for additional resources?

Resources beyond your base subscription are billed hourly for compute/memory and monthly for storage. You'll only pay for what you use beyond your included allocation.

Can I change my base subscription tier?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your base subscription at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What support is included?

All plans include 24/7 infrastructure monitoring and basic support. Premium support options are available for mission-critical applications.
